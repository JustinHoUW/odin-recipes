Grilled Asparagus

Although grilling asparagus is easy, you can still taste the vegetables' original flavors.
Fresh asparagus stalks are swiftly grilled over high heat while being sprinkled with a little oil, salt, and pepper.

These ingredients are simple yet effective in bringing out the natural flavors of the asparagus while providing a satisfying and well-seasoned dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Lightly coat the asparagus spears with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook asparagus on the preheated grill, turning often until lightly charred and fork-tender about 3 to 5 minutes.
